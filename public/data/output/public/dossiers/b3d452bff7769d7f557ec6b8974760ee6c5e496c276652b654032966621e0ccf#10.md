## Failure Mode Analysis
_grounded: yes_

1. Unverifiable delivery acceptance. Payment gating is asserted ("milestone-based disbursement controls") but no milestone, acceptance criterion, or tranche amount exists in the record — they are deferred to PSSC metadata and a legal contract not supplied.
